What Clariva Does — By Business Stage
Established Brands
STAGE 01
Revenue ₹2L–₹15L/month · Plateau or rising costs · System exists but isn't growing
  • Unit Economics Audit — True profit per order calculated after COGS, shipping, packaging, RTO & ad spend. Stop scaling products losing you money silently.
  • Funnel Leak Detection — Exact drop-off stage identified: product page, cart, or checkout — with root cause named. Convert visitors you already have instead of buying more traffic.
  • Retention Cohort Analysis — 30/60/90-day repeat purchase rates reveal if you're building a brand or renting customers. Redirect spend from new ads to cheaper reactivation of existing buyers.
  • Creative–Audience Audit — Hook Rate & CTR analysis confirms if ads actually resonate with the audience seeing them. Stop producing content nobody watches.
  • Narrative Consistency Check — Ad promise vs. landing page vs. packaging verified for coherence. Consistency builds trust; trust converts strangers into buyers.
  • RTO Reduction Strategy — COD return patterns analysed and protocols defined. A 5% reduction in returns saves thousands in wasted shipping costs monthly.
Building-Stage Brands
STAGE 02
Early sales · Ready to build a professional marketing engine · Avoiding expensive mistakes
  • Break-Even CAC Modeling — Exact maximum spend per customer acquisition calculated before losses begin. Set ad budgets with confidence, not guesswork.
  • Category Benchmark Analysis — Healthy vs. critical metric ranges for your specific niche (Skincare, Food, Apparel, etc.). Know if your 2% CVR is good or critical — measured against the best in your category.
  • Funnel Architecture — Ideal ad-to-purchase path designed with trust signals (reviews, guarantees, social proof) placed correctly from Day 1. Build a high-converting funnel the first time — not after an expensive redesign.
  • Measurement Infrastructure — 7 core metrics defined: MER, ROAS, AOV, CAC, LTV, CVR, Repeat Rate — with a daily tracking structure. Own your numbers without relying on an agency to tell you if you're healthy.
  • 30-Day Scaling Roadmap — Prioritised layer-by-layer sequence: which marketing channel to activate first and why. Scale in the right order — not randomly.
Blank-Slate Brands
STAGE 03
Product ready · No marketing system yet · Needs a blueprint to enter the market correctly
  • Category & Competitive Research — Market gap analysis to find where your brand can win. Launch with a unique angle — not as "just another brand" in a crowded space.
  • Narrative Core Construction — Your Brand Core built: who you serve, what problem you solve, why you're different — in one clear sentence. Clear identity makes marketing cheaper — you only speak to people who need you.
  • Strategic Market-Entry Blueprint — Channels, positioning, and messaging sequence for your first 30 days. Launch with a professional strategy, not hope.
  • Offer & Pricing Design — Right launch price, bundle structure, and shipping threshold for first-order profitability. A structured offer is the fastest path to initial cash flow.
  • Competitor Analysis — Direct and indirect rival weaknesses mapped to your window of opportunity. Enter knowing exactly where they're failing and how to position around it.
Local & Neighbourhood Businesses
STAGE 04
Physical stores · Local services · Cafés, salons, gyms · Footfall without high ad spend
  • Hyperlocal Demand Analysis — Neighbourhood-level search behaviour and service gaps identified. Target people within 2 km who are already looking for exactly what you offer.
  • Google Business Profile Audit — Google Maps presence optimised so you rank first for "near me" searches. Free inbound leads from people who are already ready to buy — right now.
  • Footfall Conversion Audit — The journey from board/profile visibility to in-store purchase mapped and broken points fixed. More walk-ins from the same visibility you already have.
  • Local Review Strategy — Repeatable system to collect and display reviews from happy local clients. Reviews are digital word-of-mouth — the #1 reason a local customer chooses you over a competitor.
  • Non-Digital Outreach Planning — Signage, community partnerships, and local networking plans that build reputation without Meta ad spend.
  • WhatsApp Retention System — Catalogue setup and automated follow-ups to convert a one-time walk-in into a regular. Selling to an existing client costs a fraction of acquiring a new one.
